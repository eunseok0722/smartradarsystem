RETINA-4FN

AI 4D Imaging Radar with
Object Classification

RETINA-4FN provides accurate high-resolution 4D image of target on real-time basis. This radar utilizes full of the already powerful radar chipsets with SRS’ distinctive radar antenna design methodology & software/algorithm enhancement for optimal multi-chip cascade.

Smart Radar System manufactures all radar products with its specific and patented antenna design. NUAA (Non-Uniform Antenna Array) antenna design empowers the radar performance drastically with its full radar signal transmission capacity.
