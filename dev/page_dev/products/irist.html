IRISt

Wide angle and durable
multi-purpose detector

No matter of devastating weather conditions such as rain, snow, flare, night, dust or fog, IRIS will perform as the vehicle’s eyes and ears to protect it from surrounding environments.

IRISt is an innovative product designed for industrial & machinery applications. It serves as the sensor to detect nearby human, vehicle or objects around the installed position. It is the reliable and smart guardian for the vehicle which can be used to remove blind spots, to detect front or rear crossing traffics, and to recognize objects.

IRISt possesses two radar modules to ultimately detect 180 degree from the mounted position. This well-designed, delicately confined radar will provide all the needs of markets who require high standards for safety and security.
