Smart Guard: IRIS

Smart Guard: IRIS is Smart Radar System's World-first mmWave plug-and-play safety guard package that serves as industrial & infrastructure safety guard with easy installation for any users.

It improves the safety of heavy/special vehicles' working environments with detecting obstacles around and with minimizing the blind spots owing to the widest coverage and long detection range even under challenging environments filled with thick dusts, dirties, splashing mud, fog, rain, snow, glares, no light, and etc.

Anyone can easily install and use this innovative solution to protect himself and others from an accident.
