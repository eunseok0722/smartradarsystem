LASSO IRadar

Smart Radar System's single radar module, the heart of iRadar™ (remote tank monitoring)

iRadar(TM) is a first-to-market, exclusive all-in-one design that gives users improved accuracy, cost and simplicity with no 3rd-party sensor required

Easy to install and monitor onsite or remotely.
iRadar™ is fully external to your tank.
No drilling or chemical contact required.

iRadar™ Features

  • iRadar™ Features

    - Fully integrated Satellite, GPS and radar - Long-life battery (3 – 5 years with replaceable batteries) - Class 1 Div 1, Groups C, D T4 - Installs in less than two minutes with no drilling or chemical contact required - Contactless, accurate level measurement and mounts on most tanks - Works with virtually any fluid (thick, foamy, caustic) - Optional Bluetooth screen for eye-level readings on tall tanks - Remote programming and display using iPhone

  • iRadar™ Remote Display

    - Mount display where it is convenient to view - Low-power Bluetooth wakes iRadar when user presses remote display - Display is paired to one iRadar unit - Connects up to 100 feet away - Wireless connection - no wiring required
